## PROJECTS

### Development of an Automatic Tire Pressure Control (ATPC) System and its Effect on Vehicle Traction and Fuel Consumption
* **Type:** Undergraduate Thesis
* **Supervisor:** Prof. Dr. Md. Ehsan
* **Description:** The research focuses on developing an ATPC System to optimize tire pressure for various road conditions, reducing fuel consumption and increasing vehicle traction. The system analyzes fuel efficiency and tire traction with varying pressure, load, and speed.

### tRIPSY - Sound source localizing Tri-Copter
* **Type:** ME-366 - Electromechanical System Design Project
* **Supervisor:** Dr. Kazi Arafat Rahman
* **Description:** A ROS-enabled Tri-Copter with a ReSpeaker 4-mic array and Raspberry Pi 3 for noise detection and localization. I designed a custom 3D printed body, programmed the Pixhawk 4 flight controller with Ardupilot, and implemented WiFi Telemetry with MAV-link for ground station control.

### Haptic Feedback Based Race Collaboration System for Hearing Impaired Athletes
* **Description:** An ESP-32 and NRF24L01 based low-latency communication system to synchronize races by providing haptic feedback to hearing-impaired athletes.

### Thermosyphon CPU Cooler - Modelling, Manufacturing and Performance Analysis
* **Type:** ME-310-Thermo Fluid System Design Lab Project
* **Description:** A natural convection cooler was designed and manufactured using copper and brass alloys, and its effectiveness was analyzed for up to an 80W heating load using a Peltier Module. Mathematical modeling was done using MATLAB and verified with experimental results.

### PID Based Low Temperature Heater for Medical Application
* **Description:** An Arduino Nano, Thermistor, and Ceramic Heater based device to provide localized heat to patients.
